Sonic Screw Driver Collector

This is how you Sonic!


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Screw Drivers To Get"
  2. There are four Doctors below.
    • Matt Smith
    • David Tennant
    • Peter Capaldi
    • Tom Baker
  3. Click on picture of the Doctor you wish to deliver your Sonice Screw Driver.
  4. You will add a specific amount of Screw Drivers to your total count.
  5. You win the game by matching your total count to the random number provided to you by your local Dalek.
  6. You lose the game if your total number of Screw Drivers goes above the random number and you will be Exterminated.
  7. The value of each Doctor is hidden from you until you click on it.
  8. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Doctor.
